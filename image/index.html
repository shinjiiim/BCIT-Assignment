Sholeh Zard

Recipe by: Shahrzad Jafari

Ingredients

Rice (washed and soaked) 1 cup
Water 8 cups
Sugar 2 cups
Rose water 1/4 cup
Saffron (dissolved in hot water) 1/2 teaspoon
Slivered almonds (optional) 1/4 cup
Unsalted butter 2 tablespoons
Ground cardamom (optional) 1/4 teaspoon
Cinnamon, pistachios, almonds (for garnish) As needed

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for at least 2 hours, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, bring 8 cups of water to a boil. Add rice and cook until the grains are very soft and begin to break apart.
  3. Reduce heat and stir in the sugar. Continue cooking until it dissolves completely.
  4. Add dissolved saffron, rose water, butter, and cardamom (if using). Stir well to combine.
  5. If desired, mix in slivered almonds for extra texture.
  6. Simmer gently, stirring occasionally, until the pudding thickens to a creamy consistency.
  7. Pour into bowls or a large serving dish. Let cool slightly.
  8. Garnish with ground cinnamon, pistachios, and almonds in decorative patterns.
  9. Serve warm or chilled.

Tips

  • Soaking the rice longer will make it softer and smoother in texture.
  • Dissolve saffron in hot water for maximum color and fragrance.
  • For a richer taste, you can replace part of the water with milk.
  • Decorate traditionally with cinnamon lines and pistachio/almond designs.
